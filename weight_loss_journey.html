THE SCIENCE

🔥
1. Find Your TDEE
Total Daily Energy Expenditure — the total calories your body burns in a day. Calculated using your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) multiplied by your activity factor.
BMR × Activity = TDEE
⚡
2. Estimate Activity Level
Sedentary (×1.2) → Lightly active (×1.375) → Moderately active (×1.55) → Very active (×1.725). Be honest — most people overestimate their activity level.
Honest assessment = accurate TDEE
📉
3. Calorie Deficit
Target 500–700 calories below your TDEE daily. This creates a sustainable fat loss of approximately 0.5–0.7 kg (1–1.5 lbs) per week without destroying muscle.
TDEE − 500 to 700 = Daily Goal
⚖️
4. First Law of Thermodynamics
Energy cannot be created or destroyed — only converted. Eat less energy than you burn, and your body must tap into stored fat. Physics doesn't lie.
Energy in < Energy out → Fat loss
